Got Questions?

We’ve Got Answers. Everything you need to know about Stackly and modern EdTech.

1. What makes Stackly different from other learning platforms?

Stackly focuses on personalized, modular learning paths using AI-driven feedback loops. We break down complex topics into "stacks" of micro-lessons, ensuring better retention and allowing students to learn at their own pace, unlike traditional linear course structures.

2. Which age groups and subjects does Stackly support?

Our platform is designed for learners from middle school (age 11+) through adult professional development. We currently cover STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Math), humanities, and several technical coding bootcamps.

3. How is user data and privacy protected on Stackly?

We adhere strictly to global data protection regulations (like GDPR and COPPA). All user data is anonymized, encrypted in transit and at rest, and never shared with third parties. Privacy is our top priority.

4. Can educators integrate their own content into the platform?

Yes, Stackly offers a comprehensive Creator Studio that allows verified educators to upload, format, and deploy their own modular lessons and assessments, fully integrated with our AI grading tools.

5. What are the subscription tiers and pricing options?

We offer three tiers: Basic (Free, limited content access), Premium (Monthly/Annual subscription for full library access), and Institutional (Custom pricing for schools and businesses). Details are available on our Pricing page.

6. Is Stackly accessible on mobile devices?

Absolutely. Stackly is fully responsive and optimized for all devices, including mobile phones and tablets, ensuring you can continue your learning journey anywhere, anytime.
